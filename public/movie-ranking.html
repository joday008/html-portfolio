The Best Movies According to I.B. Joel Raj

My top 3 Movies of All Time

Varnam Aayiram (2008)

Surya, an NSG official, is on a mission to rescue someone when he gets the news of his father's demise. He starts reminiscing about the bond he shared with his father and the stories related to him.

Harry Potter Film series (2001 - 2011)

The story begins when 11-year-old orphan Harry discovers that his parents were wizards and he starts his education in magic at Hogwart's School of Witchcraft and Wizardry

WALL-E (2008)

WALL•E (Waste Allocation Load Lifter Earth-Class) is the last robot left on Earth, programmed to clean up the planet, one trash cube at a time. However, after 700 years, he's developed one little glitch—a personality. He's extremely curious, highly inquisitive, and a little lonely.
